Update
The lab currently says the way to add labels to your plot is title(main='title', xlab='your x label', ylab='your y label') but this actually is a pretty bad way (since it makes an ugly overlay over the previous labels). Instead, when you originally plot, try plot(x,y, xlab='your x label', ylab='your y label'). You'll get much prettier results.
